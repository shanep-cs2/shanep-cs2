Semester Review and Evaluations

Task 1 - Complete the Student Evaluation #

Complete the student evaluations. Once you complete the evaluation email you instructor and I will add 20 to your final exam score!!

The evaluation should take no more than 5 minutes and your feedback is greatly appreciated!

Task 2 - Review your work #

Time to review for the final exam! The best way to review is to go back through all your old labs and refresh you memory. Once you have completed that you can study some of the old exams that are posted for you on canvas
