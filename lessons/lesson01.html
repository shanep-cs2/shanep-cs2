Course Introduction

Overview

For many students this will be the first time you have been exposed to more advanced Object Oriented (OO) programming concepts. This course has been designed to take someone who has never seen these concepts before to being able to understand and use them in 1 semester. In order to succeed in the course, it will be critical for you to manage your time effectively and keep up with the reading, and lab activities.
