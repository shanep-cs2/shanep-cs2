Regrade Options

Overview #

If you lost points on one of your labs all is not lost! There is a makeup lab that you can take advantage of to improve your grade. For the makeup lab you can pick ANY of your previous labs and resubmit them for a higher grade. The starter code will be an empty repository. All you need to do is copy all the code from your old lab into the lab repo, fix any issues that you had and then re-submit!

  • makeup Lab

Once you are ready to have your makeup lab graded you MUST send an email to your instructor requesting a regrade!
